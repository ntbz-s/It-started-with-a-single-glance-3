My Letter To You

My Love,

If you're reading this, I hope you know that this letter holds a little piece of my heart.

A year ago, at your sister's wedding, you were just someone I happened to notice in a room full of people. I never imagined that one day I would be writing a letter to the man I was about to marry.

Our parents may have brought us together with marriage in mind, but somewhere between our first conversations in January and all the days that followed, something unexpected happened.

I fell in love with you.

It wasn't all at once.

It happened in little moments.

In the way you listened to me. In the way you remembered small things I said. In the comfort I felt while talking to you. In the way I could be completely myself around you.

Slowly, without realizing it, you became my favorite part of the day.

I started looking forward to your messages. I started smiling at my phone. I started imagining my future, and somehow, every beautiful moment had you in it.

Love did not arrive with fireworks.

It arrived quietly.

Like sunlight slowly filling a room.

And before I knew it, my heart had already chosen you.

Thank you for becoming my safe place. Thank you for making love feel peaceful, comforting, and real.

As December comes closer, everyone talks about the wedding day — the clothes, the celebrations, the photographs.

But when I think about marrying you, I don't only think about one day.

I think about all the days after.

I think about growing older with you. Laughing with you. Supporting you. Holding your hand through every beautiful and difficult moment life brings us.

I am trusting you with my heart, my dreams, my fears, and every version of myself I will become in the years ahead.

That trust is something precious to me.

And you earned it simply by being you.

Thank you for being patient with me, for making me laugh, for listening to my endless stories, and for becoming the person I want to share everything with.

One year ago, we were strangers.

Today, you are my best friend, my safe place, and the person I cannot imagine my future without.

Inshallah, on December 24th, when we say "Qubool Hai," I will be making the most beautiful promise of my life.

I promise to choose you — not just on our happiest days, but every day after.

Because the best thing that ever happened to me was not just falling in love...

It was falling in love with you.

Forever yours,
Your soon-to-be wife ❤️

One year ago, I saw you.

Today, I love you.

And Inshallah, forever begins on December 24th. 🤍
