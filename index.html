My Letter To You

My Love,

A year ago, at Didi's sanchak, I saw you for the first time. I never imagined that a small moment would become the beginning of such a beautiful journey.

Somewhere between our first conversations and all the days that followed, I slowly fell in love with you.

It happened in little moments — the way you listened to me, remembered small things, and made me feel comfortable being myself.

You became my favourite person, the one I could share my happiness, my fears, my dreams, and all the little thoughts in between with.

When I think about marrying you, I don't only think about our wedding day. I think about all the days after — growing older together, laughing together, supporting each other, and holding hands through every chapter of life.

I am trusting you with my heart, my dreams, and my life. That trust is precious to me, and you earned it simply by being YOU.

One year ago, you were just someone I noticed in a crowd. Today, you are my best friend, my safe place, and the person I cannot imagine my future without.

Inshallah, on December 24th, when we say "Qubool Hai," I will make the most beautiful promise of my life.

I promise to choose you on our happiest days, our difficult days, and all the ordinary days in between.

I love you, meri jaan.

Forever yours,
Sadiya 🤍

I didn't know that a single moment could change my life.

But Allah had written a beautiful story for us.

And I can't wait to begin my forever with you. 🤍
