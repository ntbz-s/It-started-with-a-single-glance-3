My Letter To You

My Love,

A year ago, at your sister's wedding, I saw you for the first time. At that moment, you were just someone I noticed for a brief second. I never imagined that one day I would be writing a letter to the man I was going to marry.

Our parents may have brought us together with marriage in mind, but somewhere between our first conversations in January and all the days that followed, something beautiful happened.

I slowly fell in love with you.

It wasn't all at once. It happened in little moments , in the way you listened to me, the way you remembered the smallest things I said, and the comfort I felt knowing I could always be myself with you.

I started looking forward to your messages. I started smiling at my phone. I started imagining my future, and somehow, every beautiful moment had you in it.

Love did not arrive with fireworks.

It arrived quietly.

Like sunlight slowly filling a room.

You became my safe place. The person I could share my happiness with, my fears with, and all the little thoughts in between.

As December comes closer, everyone talks about the wedding day and the celebrations, the photographs, the memories we will create.

But when I think about marrying you, I don't only think about one day.

I think about all the days after.

I think about growing older with you, laughing with you, supporting you, and holding your hand through every chapter life brings us.

I am trusting you with my heart, my dreams, my fears, and my life.

That trust is precious to me, and you earned it simply by being YOU.

Thank you for being patient with me, for making me laugh, for listening to my endless stories, and for becoming the person I want to share everything with.

One year ago, we were strangers.

Today, you are my best friend, my safe place, and the person I cannot imagine my future without.

Inshallah, on December 24th, when we say "Qubool Hai," I will make the most beautiful promise of my life.

I promise to choose you baby, on our happiest days, on our difficult days, and on all the ordinary days in between.

Because the best thing that ever happened to me was not just falling in love.

It was falling in love with you.

Forever yours,
Your soon-to-be wife 🤍

I didn't know that a single moment could change my life.

I didn't know that a stranger could become so important.

But Allah had written a beautiful story for us.

And I cannot wait to begin forever with you. 🤍
