My Letter To You

My Love,

A year ago, at Didi's sanchak, I saw you for the first time. I never imagined that a small moment would become the beginning of such a beautiful journey.

Our parents may have brought us together with marriage in mind, but somewhere between our first conversations and all the days that followed, I slowly fell in love with you.

It happened in little moments — the way you listened to me, remembered the smallest things, and made me feel comfortable being myself.

When I think about marrying you, I don't only think about our wedding day. I think about all the days after... growing older together, laughing together, supporting each other, and holding hands through every chapter of life.

I am trusting you with my heart, my dreams, and my life. That's precious to me, and you earned it simply by being YOU.

One year ago, you were just someone I noticed in a crowd. Today, you're my dil, jaan, my safe place, and the person I imagine my future with.

Inshallah, on December 24th, when we say "Qubool Hai," I will make the most beautiful promise of my life.

I promise to choose you on our happiest days, our difficult days, and all the ordinary days in between.



Forever yours,
Sadiya 🤍
